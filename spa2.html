Oriental Spa 2

Oriental Spa 2 is El Nido's newest luxury wellness spa, featuring 40 massage stations that provide spacious and private comfort for travelers. This standalone building, thoughtfully designed by renowned designer Justin, combines modern design with traditional Eastern therapies to deliver the ultimate relaxation experience for body and mind. This newly opened spa is a must-visit wellness destination during your stay in El Nido.
