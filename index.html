H Hospitality Group, the brainchild of visionary entrepreneur Henry, is a burgeoning management company setting benchmarks in El Nido, Palawan. Amid a region witnessing a staggering 700% rise in tourism over the past seven years, the group not only influences this growth but also proudly owns shuttle services, a tour agency, and multiple hotels, further solidifying its footprint in the industry. In addition to its core hospitality services, H Hospitality Group operates several restaurants and cafés, offering diverse dining experiences that showcase the rich flavors of the region. The group is also expanding its portfolio with the development of a cutting-edge condo hotel, poised to redefine luxury living in El Nido.

Turtle Conservation Legacy

Sea Turtle Rescue & Rehabilitation Program

  • We began protecting sea turtle nests in 2016, and over the past decade, H Hospitality Group has helped release more than 60,000 sea turtles back into the ocean.
  • Starting in 2026, we received official government authorization to rescue and rehabilitate injured sea turtles. Our first successful rescue release was Mula, who had previously been unable to dive.
  • The photos feature our second rescue, a large sea turtle currently under our care. The company is committed to increasing its investment in this vital field.

Paw Rescue Project

  • A funded spay and neuter initiative, the Paw Rescue Project was conducted from September 30 to October 6 to support responsible pet care in El Nido, Palawan.
  • With the support of two volunteer veterinarians flown in from Isabela Province, local volunteers from El Nido, and employees of H Hospitality Group, a total of 382 animals were successfully spayed and neutered.

Sharing Smiles Through Gift-Giving in El Nido

  • Driven by compassion and teamwork, H Hospitality Group (HHG) brought joy to nearly 700 children in Barangay Mabini.
  • With the genuine involvement of HHG employees, children received gifts, warm meals, and heartfelt care.

A Gesture of Care for Our El Nido Elderly

  • Guided by compassion and unity, HHG extended support to 50 elderly residents of Barangay Mabini, El Nido.
  • Held for the first time, this initiative reflects the heart of HHG’s people and honors our elderly with dignity, warmth, and appreciation.

Better Classrooms Through School Brigade

  • As part of its Corporate Social Responsibility efforts, H Hospitality Group proudly participated in School Brigade 2025, supporting public schools in preparing for the new academic year.
  • HHG further strengthened its support by donating PhP100,000 worth of construction materials.

Saving Lives, One Blood Donation at a Time

  • In partnership with the Philippine Red Cross Palawan Chapter, H Hospitality Group successfully conducted its second Mass Blood Donation, collecting 48 bags of blood.
  • Recognized by the Philippine Red Cross as the only hospitality company in El Nido actively leading a mass blood donation initiative.

Community Support Network

  • With around 500 employees, H Hospitality Group is proud to contribute to the livelihood of around 500 families.

Turtle Conservation Legacy

  • We take pride in our efforts to conserve sea turtles and have saved and rehabilitated over 49,500 lives by 2024, contributing to the preservation of marine ecosystems.

Empowerment Through Brigada Eskwela

  • We recognize the importance of education in building a brighter future for our community.
  • H Hospitality Group also supports various schools by offering on-the-job training opportunities for students.

Excellence Through Community Engagement

  • H Hospitality Group proudly supports local sports as a way to strengthen teamwork, discipline, and community spirit.
  • This year, HHG will launch its own Glee Club and a Dance Club to highlight creativity through performance.

HHG Glee Club: The Sound of Music Across El Nido

  • The HHG Glee Club is an employee group within H Hospitality Group Corporation that brings everyone together through a shared love for music.
  • Making its debut on August 18, 2024, the Glee Club opened the event by singing the national anthem.
  • More than just a choir, the HHG Glee Club is a testament to HHG's commitment to work-life balance and employee engagement.

Spaying and Neutering Stray Dogs and Cats

  • In collaboration with Vet in Practice, we host weekly spaying and neutering clinics for stray dogs and cats in the local community.

Nutritional Lunch Program

  • Understanding the challenges faced by undernourished elementary school students, we have launched a program to provide nutritious lunches.

Christmas Gift Giving

  • Every Christmas, we spread joy by distributing gifts to local schoolchildren.

At H Hospitality Group, we are dedicated to forging a sustainable future for our people, community, and the environment. Our CSR initiatives are a testament to our unwavering commitment to making a positive difference in El Nido and beyond, one step at a time.
