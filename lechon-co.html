Lechon in El Nido Palawan

Lechon is a Filipino culinary treasure, traditionally served during celebrations. This slow-roasted whole pig, with its crispy golden skin and juicy, flavorful meat, is a must-try when visiting the Philippines. Now available in El Nido.
